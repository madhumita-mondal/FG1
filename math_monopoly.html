Rules
1. Roll dice and move.
2. On unowned property: solve correctly to buy it.
3. On owned property: solve correctly to pay half rent, otherwise full rent.
4. On tax square: solve correctly to pay reduced tax.
5. On chance square: solve and get reward or penalty.
6. Passing START gives $100.
7. Highest net worth wins.
